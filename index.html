WYMeditor

An open source WYSIWYM editor focusing on standards and semantics

By separating contente and presentation we’ve created an editor that’s easier to use than regular WYSIWYG-editors and that makes sure you have full control of what your site looks like.

WYMeditor generates valid strict XHTML, is supported in all major browsers and can eaily be integrated with any content management system. WYMeditor is actually already available on over 40 different platforms.
